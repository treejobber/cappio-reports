Best Practices

Top Optimization Opportunities

Reduce Unused JavaScript — Est. savings of 974 KB (mobile) / 1,116 KB (desktop)
The Wix platform loads massive JavaScript bundles on every page, most of which is unused. This is the single biggest performance bottleneck. On mobile, nearly 1 MB of JavaScript is downloaded but never executed.
Total Blocking Time: 860ms (mobile) / 1,150ms (desktop)
The main thread is blocked for nearly a full second on mobile while JavaScript executes. This means users can't interact with your page — buttons don't respond, forms don't work — until the browser finishes processing Wix's framework code.
Time to Interactive: 24.3 seconds on mobile
Your page takes over 24 seconds before it becomes fully interactive on a mobile device. Patients trying to book appointments on their phone will experience significant delays in clicking buttons or filling out forms.
