AI Readiness Recommendations

Add LocalBusiness schema to your homepage.
This is the #1 signal AI engines use to verify and cite local businesses. You have Organization schema but LocalBusiness is more specific and includes address, service area, opening hours, and geo-coordinates — all critical for local AI search results.
Add FAQPage schema to your FAQ page.
Your /faqs-contact-us/ page exists but has no FAQPage structured data. Adding this schema can trigger rich results in Google and makes your FAQ content citable by AI search engines.
Add Person schema for your team members.
Your About page lists 7 team members with roles and experience — excellent content. But without Person schema (name, jobTitle, worksFor), AI engines can't verify the real expertise behind your business. This is a missed opportunity given your strong team page.
Add Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages.
These are baseline trust signals that Google and AI engines check before citing a business. They're also legally required in most states. A template-based setup takes an afternoon.
Add Article/BlogPosting schema to your blog posts.
Your blog section exists but has no structured data. Article schema with author, datePublished, and headline helps AI engines identify and cite your content as authoritative.
